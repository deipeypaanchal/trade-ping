# Terms of Service — TradePing Bot

_Last updated: 2026-05-20. Replace with counsel-reviewed text before public launch._

## 1. Service

TradePing is a Telegram bot that, with your explicit consent, posts read-only
notifications of executed brokerage orders to Telegram groups you join. The
service uses SnapTrade as the brokerage data provider. TradePing never receives
or stores your brokerage username, password, or trading authority.

## 2. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old and legally permitted to use a brokerage
account in your jurisdiction.

## 3. Read-only access

All brokerage connections are created with SnapTrade's `connectionType: read`
mode. TradePing cannot place, modify, or cancel orders.

## 4. No financial advice

TradePing is a social-notification tool, not a financial adviser, broker, or
trading platform. Alerts are informational only, are not recommendations, and
do not constitute financial, investment, legal, or tax advice. You are solely
responsible for your investment decisions.

## 5. Privacy controls

You control the level of detail shared with the group via `/privacy
public|normal|private|off`. You can revoke all brokerage connections at any
time with `/disconnect` and delete your account by contacting the operator.

## 6. Acceptable use

You agree not to use TradePing to facilitate market manipulation, coordinated
trading, insider trading, copy-trading, or any activity that violates
applicable law or the rules of your brokerage.

## 8. Termination

The operator may suspend or terminate access at any time. You may stop using
