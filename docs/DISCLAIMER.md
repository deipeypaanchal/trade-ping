TradePing alerts are social notifications of executed brokerage orders. They
are **not** financial, investment, legal, or tax advice and are **not**
recommendations to buy, sell, or hold any security. Past trades by group
members are not indicative of future results.

Users are solely responsible for their own investment decisions. Do your own
research and consult a licensed professional before trading.

TradePing has read-only access to brokerage accounts via SnapTrade and cannot
place, modify, or cancel orders. The operator makes no warranty as to the
accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of any alert.
